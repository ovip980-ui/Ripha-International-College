About Us

RIC Nankana Sahib Campus

A proud campus of Riphah International University, serving students across Nankana Sahib, Sangla Hill, Shahkot and surrounding areas with modern facilities and experienced faculty.

We offer both Intermediate programs with free scholarship opportunities and ADP/BS pathways that lead directly to the 5th semester of Bachelor's degree programs.
